The Algorithm

Stage 1: Reverse

This means you're coming off a loss against soemone lineally behind the person whom you're about to fight, so it will pull the fighter you're about to potentially defeat backward in that sequence.

You're Fighter A. You're coming off a loss against Fighter B, your barrier. Fighter B's barrier is Fighter C, and Fighter C's barrier is Fighter D.

Stage 2: Still Attached

You won or drew in your last fight, so this attempted pass won't have reverse effects - *but,* your name is still in the active win streak of the fighter you're attempting to pass.

Imagine he/she's latching onto you as you attempt to leap through that portal. You both will go forward, but he/she will lead the sequence, and the barrier-break(s) will go to that person first and foremost.

Stage 3: All You

If you've won or drew once since your loss to a person lineally behind the person upon whom you now set your sights, and your name isn't still in the active win streak of anyone lineally behind you're upcoming opponent, you can break that barrier.

Once again, you're Fighter A. Your barrier is Fighter B. Fighter B's barrier is Fighter C, and Fighter C's barrier is Fighter D.
